About

What This System Does

End-to-end project financial control, multi-warehouse inventory tracking, procurement lifecycle management (requisition → PO → goods receipt), budget vs actual monitoring, and executive reporting — all within a single Zoho Creator application.

Budget Validation Auto-Inventory Deduction Stock Sync Overrun Workflow PO Lifecycle

Tech Stack

The entire application is built on Zoho Creator with logic implemented in Deluge scripts, forms, workflows, and reports. No external code. The data model is designed for future Zoho Inventory API sync compatibility.

Module Architecture 14 modules

01

Project Master

Central project registry with auto-generated codes, status lifecycle, and budget allocation.

02

Vendor Management

Multi-contact, multi-address vendor master with tax IDs, payment terms, performance rating, and portal access.

03

Warehouses

Multi-warehouse stock tracking with default warehouse seeding for single-warehouse deployments.

04

Inventory Master — Items

SKU-level catalog (goods/services), HSN/SAC codes, dimensions, price lists, per-warehouse stock aggregation.

05

Budget Planning

Per-project budget plans with Draft → Active → Revised → Closed lifecycle and duplicate prevention.

06

Budget Components

Dynamic budget breakdown (Labor, Materials, etc.) with allocated vs spent tracking and consumption alerts.

07

Expense Management

Expense submission with auto-approval within budget, overrun routing, and attachment support.

08

Budget Approval Workflow

Overrun approval process with PM + Finance Manager notification, budget modification, and audit trail.

09

Inventory Transactions

Stock In / Out / Adjustment / Return with per-warehouse tracking and auto-expense creation on Stock Out.

10

Transfer Orders

Inter-warehouse stock movement with paired Stock Out + Stock In transaction generation on completion.

11

Purchase Requisition

Multi-line item requisitions with 3-stage approval (Dept → Finance → Procurement) and email notifications.

12

Purchase Orders

Full PO lifecycle (Draft → Open → Closed/Cancelled), line-level discount/tax, billing/shipping addresses.

13

Goods Receipt

Accepted/rejected quantity tracking, auto Stock In creation, PO line item update, and auto-PO closure.

14

Reports & Dashboards

Executive KPIs, Budget vs Actual, stock alerts, vendor spend, inventory valuation, and scheduled daily refresh.

Key Deluge Automations 18 workflows

# Trigger Event Action
01 Project Create On Submit Generate Project Code
02 Budget Plan Submit On Submit Validate sum of components ≤ Total Budget
03 Expense Submit On Submit Budget check → auto-approve or trigger overrun workflow
04 Expense Overrun On Submit Create Budget_Approval record, send notification to PM + Finance
05 Budget Approval Status Change On Submit Update Expense status, notify requester
06 Inventory Transaction (Stock In/Out/Adj) On Submit Update Item_Warehouse_Stock, recompute Item.Current_Stock
07 Stock Out + Project On Submit Auto-create Expense record (highest-value automation)
08 Transfer Order = Completed On Submit Generate paired Stock Out / Stock In transactions
09 Goods Receipt = Open On Submit Create Stock In for Accepted Qty, update PO_Line_Item.Received_Qty
10 All Items Received On Submit Auto-set PO.Status = "Closed"
11 PO = Open On Submit Send email to vendor with PO details
12 PR Approval Stage Change On Submit Send notification to next approver in chain
13 Scheduled (Daily Cron) Midnight Budget alerts (80/90/100%), low stock alerts, KPI refresh, auto-close completed POs
